# Auto War Redfinger

Auto War Redfinger is a Discord bot designed to automate the war process for users of the Redfinger app. This bot allows users to participate in wars without needing to manually control the Redfinger app, making it easier and more efficient to engage in wars.

## Background

Saat itu aku melihat ada orang yang sedang melakukan war untuk mendapatkan phone di Redfinger dengan aplikasi auto clicker, aku berpikir hal tersebut dapat membuat device menjadi cepat rusak karena aplikasi auto clicker tersebut akan terus menerus melakukan klik pada layar, sehingga aku memutuskan untuk membuat sebuah bot yang dapat melakukan war secara otomatis tanpa harus menggunakan aplikasi auto clicker.

## Tech Stack

*   **Language:** TypeScript
*   **Library:** [Discord.js](https://discord.js.org/), [Drizzle](https://drizzle.team/) (ORM)
*   **Runtime:** [Bun](https://bun.sh/)
*   **Database:** [PostgreSQL](https://www.postgresql.org/) hosted on [Supabase](https://supabase.com/)

## Features

*   **Automated War Process:** The bot can automate the war process such redeeming code, upgrading device, and replacing device.
*   **User-Friendly Interface:** The bot provides a user-friendly interface for users to interact with and manage their war activities.
*   **Efficient War Management:** The bot helps users manage their war activities more efficiently, allowing them to focus on other tasks while the bot handles the war process.
